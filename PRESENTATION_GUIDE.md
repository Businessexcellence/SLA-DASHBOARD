## üé§ Presentation Tips

### **Timing:**
- **Total Duration:** 20-25 minutes
- **Slides:** 15 minutes
- **Live Demo:** 5-8 minutes
- **Q&A:** 5-10 minutes

### **Key Talking Points:**

**For CEO:**
- Focus on strategic value and competitive advantage
- Highlight $2M+ in prevented churn
- Emphasize operational maturity demonstration to clients
- Show future roadmap vision

**For CBO:**
- Emphasize account-level insights for client retention
- Show Top/Bottom performers for prioritization
- Discuss upsell opportunities from improvement trends
- Financial impact and ROI metrics

**For CGO:**
- Best performing accounts as case studies
- Regional performance for market expansion
- Success replication opportunities
- Growth enablement through data insights

**For CHRO:**
- Practice head accountability metrics
- Regional head effectiveness
- Training needs identification
- Talent allocation optimization

### **Demo Best Practices:**
1. **Prepare:** Open dashboard in browser before meeting
2. **Bookmark:** Create bookmarks for each view (Executive, Monthly, Account)
3. **Practice:** Run through demo flow at least twice
4. **Filters:** Show North region filter as example
5. **Engagement:** Ask leadership which account they want to deep dive on
